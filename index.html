OZ: The Great and Powerful

I am at the point in my life where i have favorites of things such as movies and perhaps find some meaning in them and attach my identity to them.

My top three movies are:

  • 2:22 (2017)
  • OZ: The Great and Powerful
  • The Parent Trap

I rewatched OZ yesterday (19/03/24) and gained a new level of respect for the movie. That respect is What i wish to impart to you. I believe this a great movie for three important reasons

  1. The Shift between worlds

    2. OZ (The Movie) does something unique in the way it handles the movement or shift between worlds. The movie starts of with a monochrome colour scheme and a 4:3 aspect ratio which changes to a more vibrant world (and the standard 16:9) when we and OZ enters the land that bears his name. It's a beautiful act of filmaking that i think you should experience.
  2. Character Development
    3. OZ (The Wizard) is asked by a young girl while in kansas to make her stand. It is almost heartbreaking especially since we, the audience, realise that he is merely a charlatan and trickster unable to do that which she asks. It is then fitting that his first ture act of magic on getting to OZ is to fix the broken legs of a china girl.
  3. The True Nature of Love

    4. Oscar Diggs (other than being a trickster) is also a charming ladies man and a change of worlds doesnt chnage him, despite his ways landing him in trouble previously, setting him on the path that led to OZ.
    He first uses his charms on a young, naive witch who is later heartbroken due to the machinations and lies of her sister which leads to her becoming the second evil witch of OZ.
    One could say (and i am) that this only happens becasue he tries to present a false version of himself to the young lady.
    On the contrary, his love interest in the movie, Glinda the Good (or perhaps Glenda) recognizes him for who he is or rather was and by so doing helps him to achieve true greatness and lead to the deliverance of the good people of OZ

    This i think is a fitting lesson in present times when we all seem to believe (after being told) that we need to present a false image of ourselves in the name of branding.

    With that being said, I hope you take some time to watch it (along with the other two!) and most importantly, Let's be Great!
